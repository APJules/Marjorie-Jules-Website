About the Firm

Jules Firm, LLC, led by Marjorie Jules, Esq., brings over 30 years of experience defending slip-and-fall, general liability, products liability, insurance matters, premises liability, automobile, construction, and municipal claims. Ms. Jules has worked at reputable Manhattan defense firms and served as in-house counsel for major insurance companies.
