About the Firm

The Jules Firm

The Jules Firm, led by Marjorie Jules, Esq., is a New York City–based practice with over 30 years of experience in defending cases involving slip-and-fall accidents, general liability, products liability, insurance matters, premises liability, automobile accidents, construction accidents, and municipal claims. Ms. Jules has worked at reputable Manhattan defense firms and served as in-house counsel for major insurance companies, bringing a well-rounded perspective to complex litigation.

The firm’s approach centers on professionalism, integrity, and diligence — result-oriented advocacy with clear communication and direct attorney access from start to finish.

At The Jules Firm, we are dedicated to providing premier defense representation across New York City. Our fundamental commitment lies in offering each client not just legal counsel, but a personalized and robust defense strategy designed to meticulously protect their legal rights and secure their best interests. We firmly believe in a client-centric approach, ensuring that every individual receives the highest caliber of attention and tailored solutions, regardless of the complexity of their situation.

Our seasoned team approaches every case with unwavering dedication, diligently exploring every facet and legal avenue to achieve the most favorable resolution possible. We are relentless in our advocacy, leaving no stone unturned as we champion our clients’ causes throughout the entire legal process.

The firm’s varied experience of our legal professionals provides a profound understanding of litigation tactics and strategies, allowing us to anticipate challenges and construct highly effective defense arguments tailored to prevailing legal precedents.
