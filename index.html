About the Firm

Jules Firm, LLC, led by Marjorie Jules, Esq., is a New York–based practice with over three decades of litigation experience. Ms. Jules began her career at Manhattan defense firms Leahey & Johnson and Lester Schwab Katz & Dwyer, then served as in-house counsel for major insurers including Travelers, GEICO, and Nationwide. She has acted as first chair at trial, conducted depositions, and handled briefing and argument in courts across all five boroughs as well as Westchester, Nassau, Suffolk, and Federal Courts. The firm’s approach is defined by professionalism, integrity, and diligent, results-oriented advocacy.
