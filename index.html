About the Firm

Jules Firm, LLC, led by Marjorie Jules, Esq., is a New York City–based practice with over 30 years of experience in defending cases involving slip and fall accidents, general liability, products liability, insurance matters, premises liability, automobile accidents, construction accidents, and municipal claims. Ms. Jules has worked at reputable Manhattan defense firms and served as in-house counsel for major insurance companies, bringing a well-rounded perspective to complex litigation.

Firm approach: professionalism, integrity, and diligence — result-oriented advocacy with clear communication and direct attorney access from start to finish.
